Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information on this web application.

Information We Collect: We do not collect any personally identifiable information. All data, including your ideas and the title of your canvas, is stored locally in your web browser's storage. We do not have access to this information.

How We Use Your Data: Since we do not collect your data, we do not use it for any purpose. The data is only used to persist your canvas content between sessions on your own device.
