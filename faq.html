Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add a new idea?

A: Simply type your idea into the input bar at the bottom of the screen and press Enter or click the "Add Idea" button.

Q: Can I change the movement of the ideas?

A: Yes, you can switch between "Flow Mode" and "Piano Mode" using the toggle button on the left of the input bar.

Q: How do I delete an idea?

A: Drag the idea you want to delete and drop it onto the trash can icon that appears in the top right corner.
